# Generate Business Case

Creates a structured business case document including executive summary, financial projections, risk assessment, and implementation roadmap.

## Parameters

- `opportunity_id`: UUID of the whitespace opportunity
- `roi_data`: Calculated ROI metrics and financial projections
- `value_tree`: Value driver hierarchy and dependencies
- `template_id`: Optional template selection for document formatting

## Output

Returns business case document with sections, charts data, and export-ready content.
